My Favorite Marine Animal

My favorite marine animal is the mantis shrimp. It is a very colorful and vibrant animal with the ability to swim fast and hit hard. When hunting, it uses club-like appendages to whack its target. It can hit so hard that it creates a tiny explosion, boiling the water, and creating a small bubble. The bubble implodes and does even more damage to its prey. This, coupled with it's very colorful looks, makes the mantis shrimp one of my favorite marine animals.
